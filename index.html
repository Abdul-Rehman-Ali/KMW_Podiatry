Why Choose Us For Your Care?


KMW Podiatry provides services in many GP Clinics around Adelaide. Keep Me Walking Podiatry has been selected to provide our caring and professional services in numerous General Practice Clinics because of our attention to detail, health department compliance, and patient care meaning you and your family enjoy the best quality care.



What makes KMW Podiatry


Different to the rest?


We service a wide range of locations which focus on various styles of treatment. We aim to provide patients access to our services under both the Bulk Billed service and Private Health Care. We endeavor to support all patients under Workcover, NDIS, Department of Veteran Affairs (DVA) and through government schemes. All our patients receive the same level of support and importance regardless of the size or requirements of their condition.
